gratia

[Ruby, Sinatra, PostgreSQL]

a journal-styled web-app,
built to help people
cultivate intentional living
through logging daily gratitude
& thoughtful reflections.

Based on the Spiritual Exercises of St Ignatius Loyola, gratia hopes to provide a safe, private space for users to sift through the memories of the day, give thanks for the big and little things, free their mind from unfree attachments and instil the habits of living well.
