kharassō

[Ruby on Rails, HTML/CSS/JS, PostgreSQL]

a web tool built to help people become their best selves through setting small, meaningful, individualized practices

kharassō is the result of my (on-going) quest in learning about and applying virtue ethics to everyday living.


The name of the app is the Greek verb kharassō (χαράσσω) meaning 'I etch, engrave, stamp, sharpen.' It is the word from which kharaktēr (χαρακτήρ, “type, nature, character”) is derived.


Our character is something “etched into” us, shaped and reshaped continually by the intentions we deliberate and small choices and practices we make and do, and our reflection on those actions.


We attain virtues, in short, through practicing them.


The gist is that at the start of every week, you reflect on your past week and add to your upcoming week a few (3 is a good number to start with) small, concrete practices that will help you grow in virtue and freer from vice.
