religio

[Ruby, OpenURI, Nokogiri]

a CLI gem that returns the names and quick facts of the top 100 world religious tradition

this tool scrapes
(retrieves, extracts and parses) data
from the external source, Patheos.com, a non-partisan, non-denominational reference library.

as an undergrad, i studied religion. i loved learning about the history and beliefs of world religious traditions, and continue to enjoy expanding on and refining that knowledge.

religion is so essential to our larger cultural, societal lives. so often though, it's linked with negative media, fueled by misconceptions and strong emotions.

i’d love to see more searching minds be informed about the factual history and ideas that shape religion and grapple with the wisdom of the ages on the basic, human existential questions.
